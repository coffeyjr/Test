ETSU Parking Services

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Permits must be displayed on the left side of the rear windshield of the vehicle, directly behind the driver and clearly visible from the rear of the vehicle.

For convertibles and Jeeps with removable tops, please place the permit on the left side of the rear bumper.

Parking permits issued in Fall 2022 are valid through the 2022-2023 academic year.

Student permits will expire on August 31, 2023. Faculty/Staff permits will expire on September 30, 2023.

Students may park in Student parking spaces/lots, which are marked gold on the curb or pavement or with signage.

Faculty & staff may park in Faculty/Staff parking spaces/lots, which are marked blue on the curb or pavement or with signage.

Undesignated lots also require parking permits and are designated for both student and employee parking.

Click here to view the detailed campus parking map.

Carpool parking is available for registered commuter students only.

Carpool applications are available here or in the Parking Services office.

At least two students must register and fill out the required application information.

Both students must live off campus, and both must have a vehicle registered with Parking Services and have obtained his/her student parking permit.
